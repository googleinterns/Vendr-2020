Welcome to Vendr Vendor Tutorial

Register your account to let costumers know you!

Go to My Business and start your business by adding a name and a description

Add a business picture, if you provide delivery service and your working hours. Don't forget to add the business picture description!

To finish, active your current location to let customers know you. After you're setup, activate your business!

Remember to update or close your business everytime you're done!
