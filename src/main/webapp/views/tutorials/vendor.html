Welcome to Vendr Vendor Tutorial

Register your account by adding your name, last name and your profile picture! It's important to add a profile picture description

Go to 'My Business' and start your business by adding a name and a description

Add a business picture, if you provide delivery service and your working hours. Don't forget to add the business picture description!

To finish, activate your current location to let customers know you. After you're setup, create your business!

Go back to 'My Business' to update or close your business everytime you need!
